About Dan

Dan Farrant was born and raised in London and now resides in Godalming, Surrey. Prior to pursuing magic as a career, Dan was a professional musician. He received his undergraduate degree from The Royal Academy of Music where he studied the double bass.

Over his 15 years as a professional magician, Dan has performed at over 1,800 events all over the UK and the world.

In his free time, Dan enjoys cooking, drinking coffee and wine, eating sushi, Mexican and Thai cuisine, reading about Roman history, studying French, playing chess, travelling, and he loves a good pun.
